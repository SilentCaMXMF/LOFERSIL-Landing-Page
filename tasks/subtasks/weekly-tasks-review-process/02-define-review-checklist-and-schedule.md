# 02. Define Review Checklist and Schedule

## Objective

Establish specific review criteria, weekly schedule, and team responsibilities for the tasks review process.

## Requirements

- Update `tasks/weekly-review-process.md` with detailed checklist
- Define weekly review cadence and timing
- Specify responsible team members and notification procedures
- Include criteria for identifying discrepancies and blocked tasks

## Implementation Steps

1. Review existing task categories and status tracking patterns
2. Define comprehensive review checklist items
3. Establish weekly schedule (day, time, duration)
4. Specify team roles and responsibilities
5. Add escalation procedures for critical issues
6. Update the process document with these details

## Verification Criteria

- [ ] Document includes detailed review checklist with specific criteria
- [ ] Weekly schedule is clearly defined (day, time, duration)
- [ ] Team responsibilities are specified
- [ ] Escalation procedures are documented
- [ ] Notification procedures are defined

## Dependencies

- Depends on Task 01 (Create Weekly Review Process Document) completion

## Estimated Time

20 minutes
