Privacy Policy

Commitment to your privacy

At LOFERSIL, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect your data.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy regulates the collection, processing and protection of personal data of users of the LOFERSIL website (www.lofersil.vercel.app). By using our website, you agree with the practices described in this policy.

LOFERSIL respects applicable data protection legislation, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Personal Data Protection Law.

3. Data We Collect

3.1 Voluntarily Provided Data

Currently, our website does not include contact or registration forms. However, if in the future we implement features that require the submission of personal data, we will collect only the necessary information for the requested service.

3.2 Automatically Collected Data

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain technical information through cookies and similar technologies:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referrer (origin website)
  • Screen resolution and other technical settings

4. Purposes of Processing

We use collected data for the following purposes:

  • Improve user experience: Analyze how the website is used to optimize design and functionality
  • Technical maintenance: Ensure correct functioning of the website and resolve technical issues
  • Security: Protect the website against threats and fraudulent activities
  • Legal compliance: Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

5.1 What are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. We use cookies to improve your browsing experience and analyze website traffic.

5.3 Cookie Management

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the website's functionality.

6. Data Sharing

LOFERSIL does not sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes. We may share data only in the following situations:

  • Service providers: Companies that help us operate the website (hosting, analytics), subject to confidentiality agreements
  • Legal obligations: When required by law or competent authority
  • Rights protection: To protect our legal rights or investigate suspicious activities

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure or destruction. These measures include:

  • Data encryption in transit (HTTPS)
  • Restricted access to data
  • Continuous security monitoring
  • Regular security updates

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Analytics data is typically retained for 2 years, after which it is automatically deleted or anonymized.

9. Data Subject Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to restriction: Restrict the processing of your data
  • Right to portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to object: Object to the processing of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us via email info@lofersil.pt or phone +351 21 123 4567.

10. Minors

Our website is not intended for minors under 16 years of age. We do not consciously collect personal data from minors under 16 years of age. If we become aware that we have collected data from a minor, we will delete that data immediately.

11. Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to other websites. This privacy policy applies only to our website. We recommend that you consult the privacy policies of the other websites you visit.

12. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Any changes will be published on this page with the date of the last update. We recommend that you consult this page regularly to stay informed about how we protect your data.
